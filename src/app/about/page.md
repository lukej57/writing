I have broad experience in software development, but I am concerned more with fundamental principles than technologies.
That's because I value clarity, and fundamentals **illuminate** while technologies **complicate**.

Clarity in my view is abstraction precisely communicated.
Abstraction creates emphasis, but risks vagueness.
Think deeply through example to retain precision.
Then communicate to integrate everything you have learned. 
The result is clarity.
